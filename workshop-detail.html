About This Workshop

This foundational workshop introduces educators to the concept of global competencies and their importance in 21st-century education. Participants will explore the four domains of global competence as defined by OECD and learn practical strategies for integrating these competencies into their curriculum.

Whether you're new to global education or looking to deepen your practice, this workshop provides essential knowledge and tools to help your students develop the skills they need to thrive in an interconnected world.

Topics Covered

  • Understanding the four domains of global competence
  • Recognizing the importance of global competencies in education
  • Exploring integration strategies for various subject areas
  • Developing assessment methods for global competencies
  • Creating globally-focused learning experiences
  • Building intercultural understanding in the classroom

Workshop Format

This interactive virtual workshop combines presentations, small group discussions, and hands-on activities. Participants will have opportunities to collaborate with peers, share experiences, and develop action plans for their own educational contexts.

Who Should Attend

This workshop is designed for:

  • K-12 educators across all subject areas
  • Curriculum coordinators and instructional coaches
  • School administrators interested in global education
  • Pre-service teachers and education students
